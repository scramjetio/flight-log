Today we are incredibly proud to open-source **FlightManual**, our premium documentation substrate designed specifically for the AI era. 

### Why FlightManual?

Traditional documentation frameworks are either locked behind expensive SaaS paywalls or look like they were designed in 2010. FlightManual bridges the gap by layering a stunning, high-conversion dark mode design system on top of Astro Starlight.

- 🤖 **AI-Native out of the box:** Automatically generates `/llms.txt` and `/llms-full.txt` during the build process, allowing any AI agent to ingest your entire documentation instantly.
- 🔍 **0ms Latency Search:** Built-in client-side search powered by Pagefind, perfectly optimized for edge deployment on Cloudflare Pages.
- 🎨 **Premium Aesthetics:** Glassmorphism components, deep dark mode, and typography that screams "State-of-the-Art."

This is the exact engine we use to host the Scramjet ecosystem docs. Check it out on [GitHub](https://github.com/scramjetio/flight-manual).
